About

Mechanical engineering student focused on designing physical systems that perform reliably in the lab and in the field. My work includes CAD & FEA-driven structural design for solid propulsion components, leading hull and mechanism design for an underwater robotic vessel, and building an Arduino-driven liquid medication dispenser tailored to users with memory impairments. I enjoy bridging CAD, rapid prototyping, and embedded control to deliver tested, manufacturable systems.

Highlights
  • Collaborating on a specialized propulsion subteam to design and fabricate the solid rocket motor forward closure for a competition rocket targeting 30,000 ft apogee.
  • Leading CAD design for hulls and central console of a hydrodynamic robotic vessel and prototyping Arduino-controlled swimming, object detection, and retrieval capabilities.
  • Led CAD and Arduino integration for an automated liquid medication dispenser supporting individuals with memory impairments.
