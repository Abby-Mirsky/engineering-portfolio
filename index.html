About

I’m an undergraduate mechanical engineer focused on designing physical systems that perform reliably in the lab and in the field. My recent work includes CAD & FEA-driven structural design for solid propulsion components, leading hull and mechanism design for an underwater robotic vehicle, and building an Arduino-driven liquid medication dispenser tailored to users with memory impairments. I enjoy bridging CAD, rapid prototyping, and embedded control to deliver tested, manufacturable systems.

Underwater Robotics Design (Personal Project)

Aug. 2025 — present
